# AI-assisted Submission Notes

This submission package includes AI-assisted planning, repository scaffolding,
test support, consistency checks, boundary-risk review, and language polishing.
The final manuscript and submission decision remain the responsibility of the
human author.

The governance files under `submission/escience2026/governance_drafts/` are
non-final drafts. They do not satisfy final submission gates:

- Conservative deadline planning is not final deadline verification.
- The repository policy draft is not the final repository decision.
- `author_final_approval.draft.json` is not a signature.

`final_submission_ready` remains false until final gate files are created in
`submission/escience2026/`, validated, and approved by the human author.
