# Limitations Box

- Local fixtures only.
- No real OpenTelemetry collector or LangSmith API.
- No real FDO registry and no real PID registration.
- RO-Crate-like local metadata only, not full RO-Crate certification.
- Local SHA-256 digest recomputation, not signature verification.
- Privacy compliance is sentinel/key scanning, not production DLP.
- Small synthetic attack corpus, not open-world adversarial evaluation.
- Venue policy, page budget, and AI-use disclosure require human verification.
