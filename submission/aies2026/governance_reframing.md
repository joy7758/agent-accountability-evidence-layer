# Governance Reframing

For AIES, the central question changes from:

```text
How can agent self-improvement evidence be packaged as FAIR scientific infrastructure?
```

to:

```text
How can agent-to-agent systems make improvement claims accountable to other agents, institutions, and human reviewers?
```

Required additions:

- Power: who can demand evidence and who can withhold artifacts.
- Control: who owns the gate report, resolver result, and package.
- Responsibility: which actor is accountable when an agent repairs or rejects a claim.
- Governance boundary: ASIEP supports review but is not a compliance system.
- Failure modes: evidence tampering, selective disclosure, and institutional capture of evidence channels.
