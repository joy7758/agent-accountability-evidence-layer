# AIES AI-use Policy Risk

AIES 2026 is treated as higher AI-use risk in M9. The M9 project brief states
that AI-generated text should not be used as directly submitted paper text.

Implication:

- Use Codex output for planning, outline, and internal critique only.
- Human authors must produce final prose.
- Human authors must verify the current AIES policy before any submission.
- AI-use disclosure must be explicit and venue-compliant.
